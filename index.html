About Heather Glenn

Heather Glenn is a musical visionary, bringing light and joy wherever her voice carries. With a unique blend of humor, heart, and harmony, Heather’s performances leave audiences inspired and uplifted. Whether breaking into song unexpectedly or delivering powerful ballads, Heather’s mission is simple: to light up the world through music.

Let There Be Light Tour

Join Heather Glenn as she takes the stage in cities across the country. Her **Let There Be Light Tour** is a celebration of song, spirit, and the power of music to transform darkness into brightness.
